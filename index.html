Why do we need explicit sexual consent?

Safe sex is not only about using physical protection. It's about consenting to what will happen in the bedroom.

Sweden is going to implement a new law that requires explicit consent before sexual contact, as a response to the #metoo-movement that dominated media in 2017.

Communication and understanding is key when it comes sex that is pleasurable for both parties. Talking about rules and boundaries before sex can be akward and uncomfortable for some. Having an app that clearly shows the rules of engagement as well as personal preferences, can remove misunderstandings and prevent unintentional bad situations.

LegalFling can also assist when things do go wrong, by putting you in contact with the right experts that can listen and help.
